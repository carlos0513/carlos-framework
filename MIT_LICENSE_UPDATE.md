## License Comparison

### Why MIT?

**MIT Advantages:**

- More permissive than Apache 2.0
- Simpler and shorter license text
- Widely used and understood by developers
- Compatible with most other open source licenses
- Minimal restrictions on use and distribution

**Key Differences from Apache 2.0:**

- No explicit patent grant (but still permissive)
- No attribution requirements beyond copyright notice
- Simpler compliance requirements for users
- Shorter and easier to understand
